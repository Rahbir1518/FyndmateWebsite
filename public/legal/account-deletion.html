Delete Your Troupe Account

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

You can permanently delete your Troupe account using either method below.

Option 1: Delete directly in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Troupe and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile tab.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

This action permanently removes your profile, matches, messages, projects, and experiences from active use.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us using the email address linked to your Troupe account.

Email: infotroupe1@gmail.com

Email account deletion request

What data may be retained

  • We remove account access and active profile data after deletion.
  • Limited audit/security records may be retained for legal, fraud-prevention, and safety obligations.

Response time

We target responses to deletion requests within 2 business days.
